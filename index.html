Got Questions?

Straight Answers.

Will my clients know they're talking to an AI?
Most won't — and it doesn't matter much either way. Your AI speaks naturally, uses your business name, and handles the conversation smoothly. The goal is to make sure every caller gets a great experience and a confirmed booking instead of voicemail.
Does it work with my existing Google Calendar or booking system?
Yes. We integrate directly with Google Calendar out of the box. If you use another system (Jane App, Vagaro, Mindbody, etc.), we'll figure out what's connectable during your setup call — most are.
What if someone asks something the AI doesn't know?
We train it on your specific services, prices, and FAQs. For anything it can't answer, it takes a message and flags it for you — or offers to have you call back. It never makes things up or gives wrong information.
How long does setup actually take?
One discovery call (20–30 min) and we build everything out. Most clients are live within 1–2 business days. Noel handles the technical side completely — you don't do much at all.
Do I need any new technology or apps?
No. If you have a phone number and Google Calendar, you're set. Everything runs in the background. You don't download anything, learn anything, or change how you already work.
What happens to my existing phone number?
We either provision a new local Vancouver number that forwards to your AI, or work with your existing number in many cases. We'll find the cleanest option on your setup call.
Is there a long-term contract?
Nope. Month-to-month on all plans. Give 30 days notice if you want out. We're confident you won't — but we never want to hold you hostage.
Can I just talk to a real person before signing up?
