The Real Problems (And Fixes)

An honest look at what's actually broken in your industry

Look, we're not going to sugarcoat this. Running a service business is chaos. Phones ring nonstop with the same questions. People ghost appointments. Your DMs are a warzone. You're doing the work of three people.

The good news? Most of this is fixable. Not with more hustle. With better systems. Specifically, robots that handle the repetitive garbage so you can actually do your job.
